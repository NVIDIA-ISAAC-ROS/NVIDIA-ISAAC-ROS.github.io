Sensors Setup

Configure one or more of the cameras:

  * :doc:`realsense_setup`
  * :doc:`zed_setup`


Calibrate your cameras depending on your type of camera and the object it is on:

   * :doc:`camera_calibration`
   * :doc:`isaac_for_manipulation_camera_calibration`
   * :doc:`amr_extrinsic_calibration`
